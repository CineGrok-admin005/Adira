This involved building a robust database queue system so she never wastes content, pacing her posts out evenly (3 times a day), and updating her AI prompts to align with 2026 Instagram Social SEO standards.

---

## 🏗️ Architectural Upgrades & Rationale

### 1. The Supabase Content Backlog
**Why:** Previously, if 5 major events happened in a single day, Adira reported one and immediately deleted the other 4. This wasted massive growth opportunities. Furthermore, if the Hugging Face GPU limit was reached, a post failed permanently. 
**What:** We built a `content_backlog` table in Supabase. It acts as a safety queue. Adira now saves unused milestones and news stories into this queue. If she runs on a "dry day" (no new organic data), she pulls from the queue. We implemented a strict **Time-To-Live (TTL)** (48 hours for news, 7 days for milestones) so she never posts stale content.

### 2. The 3-Post Drumbeat
**Why:** Algorithmic momentum relies on consistency, not spikes. 
**What:** We updated her cron jobs to wake her up at 8:00 AM (Platform Growth), 1:00 PM (News Commentary 1), and 7:00 PM (News Commentary 2). 

### 3. Social SEO Enforcement
**Why:** Instagram's 2026 algorithm heavily penalizes hashtag spam (e.g., blocks of 15-30 tags) and prioritizes keyword-rich captions.
**What:** We re-wrote Adira's core system prompts to strictly limit hashtags to 3 hyper-specific tags and forced her to naturally weave highly-searched keywords directly into the caption bodies.
